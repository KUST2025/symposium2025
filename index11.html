2025 International Symposium on Algebraic q-Structures and Geometric Function Theory

To foster academic collaboration among researchers in geometric function theory and algebra, and to promote research excellence, we are pleased to announce the 2025 International Symposium on Algebraic q-Structures and Geometric Function Theory, which will be held from September 1 to 9, 2025, in Kohat, Pakistan. This symposium is organized by the Department of Mathematics at Kohat University of Science and Technology.

We cordially invite you to participate and deliver a keynote presentation. The details are as follows:

I. Symposium Schedule

  • Registration
    Date/Time: September 1, 2025 (9:00 AM–6:00 PM)
    Venue: KUST Conference Hall
  • Symposium Sessions
    Dates: September 2–8, 2025
    Venue: Department of Mathematics, KUST
  • Departure: September 9, 2025

II. Expenses
